Notes

1) <h1></h1>
- Shows the possible biggest headline.
2) <strong></strong>
- Important text is being emphasized on a paragraph.
3) <mark></mark>
- Keyword is being visible on each paragraphs.
4) <sup></sup>
- Superscript text is being used in showing ordinal numbers, temperature, etc. E.g. 1st,120oF
5) <abbr ="..."></abbr>
- Contains the meaning or full text of an abbreviated term. E.g. HTML
