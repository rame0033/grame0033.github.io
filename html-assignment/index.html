About Me

Gian was raised from the Philippines. Graduated BA Major in Broadcasting at Colegio de San Juan de Letran -Manila. During my 1st year of corporate life, I worked as a production assistant on an independent company which consists of only 10 people in a production. Several months later, they discovered that I have a potential on being a video editor, so they let me, but I still assist during video shoots.

When the pandemic hit, I have to find other opportunities that would improve my abilities in media design since it became in demand. I was able to be hired as a post-production assistant in where I assisted in doing designs, animating videos, and editing. In 2023, I enrolled in Algonquin College with the Interactive Media Design program to gain more knowledge and improve more skills.
