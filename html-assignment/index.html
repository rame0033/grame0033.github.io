About Me

Raised from the Philippines. Graduated BA Major in Broadcasting at Colegio de San Juan de Letran -Manila. During his 1st year of corporate life, he worked as a production assistant on an independent company which consists of only 10 people in a production. Several months later, they discovered that Gian have a potential on being a video editor, so they let him, but still assist during video shoots.

When the pandemic hit, Gian have to find other opportunities that would improve his abilities in media design since it became in demand. He was able to be hired as a post-production assistant in where he assisted in doing designs, animating videos, and editing. In 2023, he enrolled in Algonquin College with the Interactive Media Design program to gain more knowledge and improve more skills.
